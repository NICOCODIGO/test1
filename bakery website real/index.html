About Us

Pan Bakery brings the world's bread traditions to your table through its baking passion. The bakery operates with a straightforward concept which involves open oven baking to let customers experience freshly baked bread straight from the oven. The team creates a wide range of products that include breads and cakes as well as savory items. The family-run business maintains a supportive environment for its members and local residents through daily breakfast service and welcoming atmosphere. The business maintains its constant objective to create perfect mornings for everyone.
